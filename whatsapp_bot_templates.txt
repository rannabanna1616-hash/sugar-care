==========================================
WHATSAPP AUTO-REPLY BOT TEMPLATES
==========================================
Copy and paste these templates into your WhatsApp Business App's 'Quick Replies' or 'Auto-reply' settings.

▶ GREETING MESSAGE (When a customer first sends their order details)
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Thank you for your order 🙏

Your order has been received successfully.

Our team will contact you soon for confirmation.

Delivery time: 3–5 days 🚚


▶ FOLLOW-UP MESSAGE (If the customer doesn't reply after some time)
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Hi 👋 just checking!

Do you want to confirm your order?

🔴 Limited stock available today.
